# Pricing Policy

Our platform offers three main subscription pricing tiers, as well as customized options for larger organizations:

## Subscription Tiers
1. **Starter Plan**: $49 per month. Includes 5 user seats and up to 10,000 API requests per month. Support response time SLA is 48 hours.
2. **Standard Plan**: $299 per month. Includes 15 user seats and up to 100,000 API requests per month. Support response time SLA is 12 hours.
3. **Pro Plan**: $999 per month. Includes 50 user seats and up to 500,000 API requests per month. Support response time SLA is 4 hours.

## Non-Profit Discounts
We are proud to support registered 501(c)(3) organizations, charities, and non-profit institutions. We offer a **30% discount on the Standard Plan** (reducing the cost to $209.30/month) for registered non-profits. This discount must be manually validated by our billing team upon receiving proof of 501(c)(3) registration.

## Mid-Cycle Upgrades and Pro-Rata Billing
If an account upgrades its subscription plan or adds additional user seats mid-cycle, we compute a pro-rata charge for the remainder of the billing period:
- **Seat Addition**: Charged pro-rata based on the number of days remaining in the monthly cycle.
- **Plan Upgrade**: The customer is billed for the difference between the new plan and the old plan, pro-rated for the remaining days of the current month.
- The new rate and seat limit take effect immediately upon payment confirmation.

## Enterprise Custom Pricing
For customers requiring more than 50 seats, dedicated servers, custom data residency, or higher API rate limits, we offer custom Enterprise agreements. Enterprise pricing starts at $2,000/month and requires a minimum 12-month contract.
